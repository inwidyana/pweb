Biro Kepegawaian - Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika's Website

I developed this site as part of my internship as a Fullstack Web Developer at Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika.

The goal of the project was to develop as site that would serve as a main internal staff's memo.

Which would allow its administrator to publish article, news and photos.

    Feature included:

  • Responsive user interface compatible for tablet, mobile and desktop.
  • Database model.
  • Administrator account management system.
  • Article management page(Content Management System).
  • Open source editor capable of writing news-standard articles.
